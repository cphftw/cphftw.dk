The Copenhagen startup scene. United.

#cphftw is a grass root initiative to unite the startup community in Denmark.

The goal is to bring the entire community together and work out ways to improve entrepreneurial ecosystem in Denmark centered around Copenhagen.

#cphftw aims to integrate a strong focus on building relationships, trust, knowledge and awareness in the danish startup community.
