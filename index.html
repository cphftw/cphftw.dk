The Copenhagen startup scene. United.

#cphftw is a grass root initiative to unite the startup community in Denmark.

The goal is to bring the entire community together and work out ways to improve entrepreneurial ecosystem in Denmark centered around Copenhagen.

#cphftw aims to integrate a strong focus on building relationships, trust, knowledge and awareness in the danish startup community.

#cphftw brings people together to do great things.

It’s a movement and everyone's invited.

What is CPHFTW?

#cphftw is a bottom-up movement with the purpose of putting Copenhagen on the global startup map. The movement has broad support from some of the biggest investors, entrepreneurs, politicians and community builders in Denmark. Copenhagen is already the most livable city in the world. Why not also make it a place, where we re-define what it means to build really awesome world class tech companies too?

#cphftw works with passionate and talented entrepreneurs, and provides on-going support to startups and startup initiatives by shaping the entrepreneurial landscape in Copenhagen and Denmark.

The Manager role

Working with the entrepreneurial crew behind and to a great extent driven by your own engagement, you will have a key role to further shape and develop the entrepreneurial landscape.
